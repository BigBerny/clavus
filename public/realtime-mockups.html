Picking Guide

Layout: 1a (voice-dominant) vs 1b (minimal) vs 1d (floating pill)

Recording: 2a (inline compact) vs 2b (orb + live text) vs 2c (strip + auto-send badge)

Send: 3a (auto-send + cancel) vs 3b (review first) vs 3c (configurable toggle)

Options: 4a (visible chip) vs 4b (long-press) vs 4c (swipe drawer)

Text editing: 5a (expand inline) vs 5b (overlay card)

Mix and match: e.g. "1a + 2c + 3c + 4a + 5a"
